1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees farenheit
2. Put the butter and sugar into the bowl and stir well for 3-5 minutes
3. Add the flour, salt, baking powder or baking soda and stir for another 5 minutes until it is thin
4. Crack the eggs and add them into the mix and stir well
5. After mixture is nice and thin get oven trays
6. Spray oven trays with oil very lightly
7. Make cookie shapes on the trays with the mix
8. Put the cookies in the oven and bake at 375 degrees farenheit for 10-12 minutes
9. Once cookies are done nicely, eat and enjoy!
